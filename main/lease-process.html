Lease Process

Our comprehensive approach to securing your ideal property with integrated services

A Comprehensive & Integrated Process

At CAPPADOR Global, we've developed a streamlined property leasing process that combines our expertise in real estate, security, construction, and facility management. Our team of professionals guides you through each step, from initial consultation to move-in day, ensuring a seamless experience that meets your specific needs.

Below is our comprehensive six-step process that has helped numerous clients secure premium properties with integrated services across Nigeria's prime locations.

1

Initial Consultation

We begin by understanding your property requirements, budget, and location preferences. This helps us identify the most suitable properties from our diverse portfolio of residential and commercial spaces, along with the necessary security and facility management solutions.

What to prepare:

  • Your property type and usage requirements
  • Preferred location(s) in Nigeria
  • Space requirements (size, layout, features)
  • Budget range for rent and additional services
  • Security and facility management needs
2

Property & Services Shortlisting

Based on your requirements, we'll create a curated list of available properties that match your criteria. Each option comes with detailed information about the property, its location, and available integrated services.

What you'll receive:

  • Detailed property specifications
  • High-quality photos and floor plans
  • Location analysis and accessibility information
  • Security and facility management options
  • Construction and renovation possibilities
3

Guided Property Viewings

Schedule viewings of your shortlisted properties at your convenience. Our property consultants will accompany you to each location, providing expert insights and demonstrating available security and facility management solutions.

What to expect:

  • Flexible viewing schedules
  • Expert guidance from our property consultants
  • Security system demonstrations
  • Facility management overview
  • Construction and renovation consultations
4

Service Package Negotiation

Once you've selected your preferred property, we'll negotiate the best possible terms for your lease and integrated services. Our team has extensive experience in securing favorable conditions for our clients.

What we negotiate:

  • Monthly rent and annual increases
  • Security service packages
  • Facility management solutions
  • Construction and renovation terms
  • Cleaning service agreements
5

Documentation & Legal Review

Our legal team will handle all necessary paperwork and ensure all agreements protect your interests. We provide comprehensive legal support throughout the entire process.

Documents we prepare/review:

  • Lease agreement and all associated schedules
  • Security service agreements
  • Facility management contracts
  • Construction and renovation agreements
  • Cleaning service contracts
6

Move-In & Service Setup

After signing the agreements, we'll coordinate the move-in process and set up all integrated services. Our relationship continues even after you've moved in, with ongoing property management and service support.

How we help:

  • Security system installation and setup
  • Facility management implementation
  • Construction and renovation coordination
  • Cleaning service commencement
  • Ongoing support throughout your tenancy

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the entire lease process typically take?

The typical lease process takes between 4-8 weeks from initial consultation to move-in. This timeframe can vary depending on several factors, including the complexity of the lease negotiations, required construction work, security system installation, and how quickly you can provide the necessary documentation.

For clients with urgent property requirements, we also offer an expedited process that can secure a property in as little as 2-3 weeks for certain locations.

What types of properties and services do you offer?

We offer a comprehensive range of properties and services:

Properties:
1. Residential properties
2. Commercial spaces
3. Office buildings
4. Mixed-use developments

Integrated Services:
1. Security solutions (24/7 guards, surveillance, access control)
2. Construction and renovation services
3. Facility management
4. Professional cleaning services

What security and facility management services are included?

Our properties come with comprehensive security and facility management solutions, including:

1. 24/7 security personnel and surveillance
2. Access control systems
3. Regular facility maintenance
4. Professional cleaning services
5. Emergency response protocols

We can customize service packages based on your specific requirements and property type.

What additional costs should I expect beyond the base rent?

Beyond the base rent, tenants typically need to account for:

1. Security service fees
2. Facility management charges
3. Construction and renovation costs (if applicable)
4. Cleaning service fees
5. Utilities and maintenance
6. Security deposit (usually 3-6 months' rent)

We provide a detailed breakdown of all costs during our initial consultation to ensure complete transparency.

What documentation is required for the lease process?

The standard documentation required includes:

1. Valid identification documents
2. Proof of income or business registration
3. Bank statements or financial references
4. Previous rental history (if applicable)
5. Security clearance documentation

For commercial properties or construction projects, additional business documentation may be required.

Is there ongoing support after I've moved in?

Absolutely! Our relationship doesn't end once you've signed the lease. We provide ongoing support throughout your tenancy, including:

1. 24/7 property management support
2. Regular security system maintenance
3. Facility management services
4. Construction and renovation support
5. Professional cleaning services
6. Emergency response services

Our integrated approach ensures you receive comprehensive property and service support throughout your tenancy.
